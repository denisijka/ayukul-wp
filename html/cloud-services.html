Cloud Services

Elevate Your Transformation and Cloud with Us
Ayukul Technologies provide a smoother transition for your company’s migration to cloud services. We keep the cost minimum and impact maximum so that your return on investments are justified.
Managed Cloud provides single hand management for private, public, and hybrid clouds, so that your prime focus remains on innovation. With decades of experience in managing IT environments, we implement best practices not only to monitor but also to manage cloud infrastructure to minimize risk and adhere to service levels.

While upgrading to cloud migration, an enterprise has the option to adopt these services through several CSD (Cloud Service Delivery) models:

  • Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS)
  • Platform as a Service (PaaS)
  • Software as a Service (SaaS)

Similarly, an enterprise (small, medium, or large) can select from these models:

  • Private Cloud
  • Public Cloud
  • Hybrid Cloud

Why Cloud Services?

A Specialized Delivery Model
MCaaS is one of the specially designed cloud delivery models. It also enables enterprises to advantage the computing resources on-demand. However, the specific model makes it much easier for companies to benefit a variety of services without worrying about implementation and management.
Extended Other Cloud Models
The cloud delivery model enables enterprises to run applications and store critical data instead of constructing an on-premise infrastructure. MCaaS also enables enterprises to extend other cloud models by outsourcing services, management, and support.
Cost Saving
By deploying your cloud managed services, you reduce the expensive network maintenance cost. Hiring a full-time technical team to manage simple networks can costly for small or medium businesses. However, outsourcing to a managed services provider like Ayukul, can save a majority percentage of your total expense rather than spending it on an in-house team.
Customized Cloud Services
Enterprise application software providers form partnerships with cloud service providers to deliver a customized cloud service solution. They leverage this specific model to allow the customization of the software products accordingly. The partners moreover improve the quality of the services by delegating resources along with excellent technical support.
Robust Infrastructure
Cloud MSPs like Ayukul offer a robust network infrastructure with round the clock administration. As decided on the SLA, a cloud service provider shall monitor and scan the networks for requirements, security, and more. MSPs can also work on existing business practices working concurrently to manage your system and collaborate towards a common goal.
Disaster Recovery
We have designed and redesigned numerous networks and data centers with resiliency to remain in the continuity of the business. With cloud managed services, your data is safe and secured across all applications. In the event of a disaster, your business operations can run smoothly and without any hiccup.
